Installing

The only thing you need to do to install and activate FubuMVC.CodeSnippets in a LINK TO PROJECT 'FubuMVC' application is to just get the FubuMVC.CodeSnippets.dll into your application. The easiest way is to just use Nuget: 
Install-Package FubuMVC.CodeSnippets
